Workflow Automation

The system automated complex approval processes across departments:

1

Document Submission

Employees submit documents through the portal with required metadata

2

Automated Routing

System routes document to appropriate approvers based on type and department

3

Approval Process

Approvers receive notifications and can approve/reject with comments

4

Final Processing

Approved documents are automatically filed and indexed in the system

5

Notification

Submitter receives notification of final status and next steps

System Integrations

The solution was integrated with the client's existing enterprise systems:

SAP ERP Microsoft SharePoint Workday HRIS Active Directory Microsoft Teams
